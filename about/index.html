About me

I am an assistant professor in the Department of Psychology, Medical University of Gdańsk, Poland. I do research in auditory cognitive neuroscience. In my work I try to figure out how the brain makes people hear sounds.

I also did some work in pain psychology. My PhD thesis on predictors of chronic low back pain (in Polish) can be found [here].
